RULES

1
You have to be fit to participate. If you are not fit the judges have the right to disqualify you
2
You participate at your own risk. In the event of an accident, injury or death from
participation, Kakamega relay will not be held responsible
3
The judges’ word is final.
4
Understand and follow participation rules, if you don’t, you will either be disqualified or
denied price money if you win.
5
Do not take drugs of any kind including medicine. Participants will be tested and anybody
found to be positive with performance enhancing drugs will be banned from Kakamega Relays
for life.
6
Participate fair and Win fair. If you cheat in any way or you win unfairly, you will be
disqualified, banned for 10 years and you will not be entitled to any price money.
7
If you see something, say something to authorities. Look out for everybody and let all of us
have fun and be safe.
8
There has to be a minimum of five (5) participants (racers) for an event to take place.
9
Spaces are limited. You have to register on the website in advance to compete or participate.
No spots are guaranteed on race day.
10
Registration Fee: Learners/students Sh.50. Adults Sh.100. Fee may be waived under special
circumstances. Senior participants in all age categories are exempt for registration fee
